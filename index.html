What exactly is a "blog"

A blog (a truncation of "weblog") is a discussion or informational website published on the World Wide Web consisting of discrete, often informal diary-style text entries (posts). ... Until 2009, blogs were usually the work of a single individual, occasionally of a small group, and often covered a single subject or topic.

What exactly is a "vlog"

A video blog or video log, sometimes shortened to vlog (/vlɒɡ/), is a form of blog for which the medium is video, and is a form of web television. ... Vlog entries often combine embedded video (or a video link) with supporting text, images, and other metadata.
